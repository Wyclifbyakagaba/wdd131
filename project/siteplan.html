Explore Uganda Site Plan

Site Name

Explore Uganda

This name was chosen because it represents a tourism website that helps users discover beautiful places, attractions, and travel experiences in Uganda.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about tourist destinations in Uganda, including national parks, cities, wildlife, and cultural sites. It will also guide visitors on travel tips and recommendations.

Scenarios

  • What are the best tourist attractions to visit in Uganda?
  • When is the best time to travel to Uganda?

Color Scheme

Green (#2e7d32): Used for header, headings, and footer.

Yellow (#f4c430): Used for accents, borders, and highlights.

Typography

Roboto: Used for all headings and body text for a clean and modern look.
